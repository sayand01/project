About EV Charging Station Finder

EV Charging Station Finder is a web application that helps electric vehicle (EV) drivers find charging stations near their location. Our goal is to make it easy and convenient for EV drivers to plan their trips and find charging stations along the way.

How It Works

EV Charging Station Finder uses the Google Maps API to display the location of nearby charging stations on a map. Users can enter their location in the search bar and click the search button to see a list of nearby charging stations. The app also provides information about the charging station, including the type of charger, availability, and pricing (if available).
